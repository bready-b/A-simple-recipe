Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegatables, or meats.

Preperation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes
    2-3 large eggs
    Salt, to taste
    Pepper, to taste
    1 tablespoon of butter or oil
    Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs
